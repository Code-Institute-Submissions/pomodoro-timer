About Pomodoro Timer

The Pomodoro timer is a well-known productivity interval that has been shown to improve your productivity. It gives you a prescribed interval of 25 minutes of work followed by a 5-minute break. After 4 work intervals, there is a 15-minute break. To start the timer click on the buttons under the green circle.

Custom Timer

While 25 minute timer works great for most people, you might have a situation where adjusting it would match your current needs. We got you covered! Just enter the amount of minutes you want into the field above the green circle and hit enter either on keyboard or button to start it.

Progress Tracking

Keep track of how many 25 minute segments you worked for. Enter the date and with each completed 25 minutes, click on the tomato to turn it red.
